ABOUT ME

Let Me Tell You About Myself 🌊👋🏽

I'm Amena Syed and welcome to my digital space! I'm an incoming second year Biomedical Engineering student at the University of Waterloo keen on contributing to hardware and software advancements in medical technology as well as UI/UX development.

Currently, I'm pursuing a full-time co-op as a Research and Development Assistant at MSAM where I design, manufacture and test 3D printed devices for medical diagnosis.

I'm passionate about designing products and experiences that elevate the user's real-world interactions. My strong interest in human ergonomics has resulted in me placing emphasis on both psychological and physiological principles in systems design. I'm always looking to grow as an engineer by learning and engaging in new roles and technologies.

In my spare moments, you can find me on the lookout on Pinterest for new dessert recipes, binging my next Netflix series or engaged in a family game of Carrom.
