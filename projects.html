March - April 2021

Iterative Design, Wireframing

TreadScale - Praxis Ideation Challenge

TreadScale is a commercializable weight measurement device used to safely and efficiently monitor body-weight of individuals living with Spinal Cord Injury. At a high level, the device consists of two ski-like pressure sensing platforms paired to a smartphone app which stores, measures and displays the user's weight at a click of a few buttons.

October 2020

Isolation

Isolation is a 2-player strategy game with the objective being to isolate your opponent's game piece so they are unable to make any further moves. This project was coded in C# using 1/2D arrays, loops, static methods and a basic board game interface.

February - July 2020

STEMComp 2020

Co-Organizer

STEMComp2020 challenged students across Ontario to explore their passion for STEM using creative problem solving. Due to the onset of COVID-19, the competition ran remotely and thus, students were asked to uncover a problem, theorize how they could use STEM to solve that problem, and present their solution in a 5-minute video.
